循环群的性质与分类 Properties and Classification of Cyclic Groups

2025-01-05
群论 Group Theory 基础 Basics

今天深入学习了循环群的概念。循环群是最简单但又极其重要的一类群。 一个群G称为循环群，如果存在元素a∈G使得G = ⟨a⟩ = {aⁿ | n ∈ ℤ}。

Today I delved into the concept of cyclic groups. Cyclic groups are the simplest yet extremely important class of groups. A group G is called cyclic if there exists an element a∈G such that G = ⟨a⟩ = {aⁿ | n ∈ ℤ}.

定理：所有循环群都是阿贝尔群 Theorem: All cyclic groups are abelian
证明： Proof: aᵐ · aⁿ = aᵐ⁺ⁿ = aⁿ⁺ᵐ = aⁿ · aᵐ

重要性质：有限循环群Cₙ与整数模n加法群ℤ/nℤ同构。 无限循环群与整数加法群ℤ同构。

Important property: Finite cyclic group Cₙ is isomorphic to the additive group of integers modulo n, ℤ/nℤ. Infinite cyclic groups are isomorphic to the additive group of integers ℤ.
